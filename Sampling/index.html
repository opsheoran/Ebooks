Preface

This web-book "Theory of Sample Surveys" is designed as a comprehensive resource for students and practitioners of statistics. It covers the fundamental principles and advanced techniques of sampling, from simple random designs to complex multi-stage surveys.

The goal is to provide a rigorous mathematical treatment of sampling theory while maintaining a clear, intuitive understanding of the underlying concepts.
